Basic Musical Terms

  • Note — denotes a sound of a specific pitch, the basic unit of music.
  • Melody — a sequence of sounds that creates a musical thought or phrase.
  • Rhythm — the organized alternation of sounds and pauses over time.
  • Tempo — the speed of performing a musical piece, measured in beats per minute (BPM).
  • Measure — a rhythmic unit that defines the number of beats in music.
  • Tone — the fundamental pitch of a sound or note.
  • Pause — a break or silence between sounds in a musical work.
  • Harmony — the combination of different sounds or chords that are played simultaneously.
  • Dynamics — the volume of music, from quiet to loud.
  • Timbre — the characteristic color or quality of a sound that allows us to distinguish different instruments or voices.
